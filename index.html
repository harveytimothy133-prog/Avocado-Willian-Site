Ultra Avocado Sandwich 
The Super Avocado Ultra Sandwich is a high-protein, nutrient-dense powerhouse that elevates the classic avocado toast 
  1. Prep the Base: Toast your bread until golden-brown. Spread a thick layer of seasoned avocado mash onto both slices.
  2. Layer Textures: Place the crispy bacon (or tempeh) on the bottom slice, followed by the sliced hard-boiled egg.
  3. Add Zest: Top with pickled onions and fresh sprouts to cut through the richness.
  4. The Finish: Drizzle with sriracha mayo and add the seasoning.
  5. Assemble: Close the sandwich, press down gently, and slice diagonally.
